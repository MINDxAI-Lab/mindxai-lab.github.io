  <section class="news-highlight">
    <h2>Human-Autonomy Teaming Collaboration</h2>
    <p>
      At the meeting, the MINDxAI Lab joined collaborators from Oregon State University (lead institution) and University of Florida to share ongoing research on human-autonomy teaming, with a focus on the design, measurement, and empirical analysis of effective human-autonomy collaboration.
    </p>
    <p>
      The project reflects the lab's broader interest in modeling human state, cognition, performance, and decision-making to support safer and more effective AI-enabled systems.
    </p>
  </section>
