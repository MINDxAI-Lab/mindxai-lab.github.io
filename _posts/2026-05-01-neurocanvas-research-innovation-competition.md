<p><b>From Yan:</b></p>

<blockquote>
<p>
I am honored to share that our work received Third Place 🏆 at the University of Louisville Department of Industrial &amp; Systems Engineering Research &amp; Innovation Competition — Spring 2026 🎉🎉🎉
</p>

<p>
Our project, NeuroCanvas 🧠, explores how multi-channel EEG signals can be reformulated as dense visual representations for VLLM-powered seizure detection. By converting EEG signals into compact visual canvases, our work aims to support more accurate and robust seizure detection.
</p>

<p>
I am grateful to my co-author from the MINDxAI Lab, Moajjem Hossain Chowdhury, and my PhD advisor, Dr. Yunmei Liu, for their contributions and support throughout this work 💪🏻. I also want to thank the UofL Department of Industrial &amp; Systems Engineering for providing an inspiring research environment and hosting this competition.
</p>
</blockquote>
