        This poster presents recent MINDxAI Lab research examining how varying levels of autonomous vehicle aggressiveness influence human drivers' cognitive state and behavioral responses in mixed traffic.

    <section class="poster-card">
      <h2>Poster 2 | Prior Collaboration with NCSU ITRE</h2>
      <h3>Driver Behavior Analysis at Alternative Intersection Corridors Through Driving Simulators</h3>
      <p>
        This work was developed during Dr. Liu's Ph.D. in collaboration with the Institute for Transportation Research and Education at North Carolina State University. It investigates how alternative intersection corridor designs affect driver behavior and safety-relevant responses using high-fidelity driving simulator experiments.
      </p>
      <ul class="session-details">
