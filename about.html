ABOUT MINDxAI LAB

The Modeling Interaction, cogNition, and Decision-making x AI (MINDxAI) Lab is directed by Dr. Yunmei Liu in the Industrial and Systems Engineering Department at the University of Louisville.

Our research develops human-centered AI and human-in-the-loop systems by modeling human interaction, cognition, decision-making, workload, situation awareness, and performance. We combine experimental studies, wearable sensing, physiological data, behavioral analytics, machine learning, and statistical modeling to understand how people work with complex technologies.

The lab's application areas include healthcare, transportation, automated driving, aviation, robotics, and intelligent adaptive systems. Across these domains, we aim to design AI-enabled systems that account for human strengths and limitations and improve safety, usability, and effectiveness.
